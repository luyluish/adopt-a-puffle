Adopt A Puffle

Puffles are lovely creatures you can play with. How about adopting one that fits with your personality?

Before you adopt your companion, here's a brief history on Puffles

We don't know the exact moment they started appearing here on the island, but since then, they've become our natural friends. At first they were very timid, and ran away to the woods if we got close to them, however as we started feeding and playing with them, now they are our best friends!

Puffles had many different concepts in the early stages of Club Penguin, before they could reach the design we know now (the fluffy ball things). Notice how they all have a similarity: The fur, in order to contrast the penguins' "skininess".

In order to take care of your new friend, here's some things you can do to make them happy:

  • Take them for a walk around the island
  • Feed them with their favorite food (each species has a different one)
  • Play alongside them in minigames (they like to go with you!)
  • Buy some toys so they can play when you're out
